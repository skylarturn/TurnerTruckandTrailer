Who we are

Turner Truck and Trailer is a team of dedicated road service mechanics focused on fast, professional repairs and exceptional customer service. We understand the importance of uptime and provide efficient solutions with safety and reliability in mind.

Our technicians are available around the clock to diagnose issues, make repairs on-site, and coordinate towing or shop work as needed. We serve long-haul drivers, local carriers, and owner-operators with the same commitment to quality.

We pride ourselves on transparency, clear communication, and workmanship — every job is handled with care so you can get back on the road with confidence.
