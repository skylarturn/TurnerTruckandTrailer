What We Do

At Turner Truck and Trailer LLC, we offer a range of services to keep your trucks and trailers in top condition:


  1. Air Conditioning Services: Ensure driver comfort in all weather conditions with our expert air conditioning services. We diagnose and repair A/C systems, ensuring your drivers stay cool and focused on the road.
  2. Brake Repairs: Safety is our priority. Trust us for reliable brake repairs, addressing issues promptly to maintain the stopping power of your vehicles.
  3. Lighting Solutions: From headlights to taillights, our technicians handle lighting repairs efficiently, ensuring your trucks and trailers are visible and compliant with safety regulations.
  4. Coolant Systems Maintenance: Prevent overheating issues with our coolant system services. We keep your engines operating at optimal temperatures for enhanced performance.
  5. Computer Diagnostics: Leveraging advanced technology, we offer precise computer diagnostics to identify and address issues affecting the performance of your vehicles.
  6. Suspension Repairs: Smooth out the ride with our suspension repair services. We address issues promptly to ensure stability and control on the road.
  7. Starters and Alternators: Count on us for reliable repairs and replacements of starters and alternators, ensuring your vehicles start up consistently.
  8. Fifth Wheel Services: Our technicians are skilled in addressing fifth wheel issues, ensuring proper coupling and uncoupling for your trailers.
  9. Hydraulic System Repairs: For efficient hydraulic systems, our team provides expert repairs, keeping your vehicles equipped for various applications.
  10. Liftgate Maintenance: We specialize in liftgate repairs, ensuring the smooth operation of these essential components for loading and unloading.
  11. Rollup Doors and Swing Doors: Trust us for the repair and maintenance of rollup and swing doors, ensuring secure and efficient access to your cargo.
  12. Full-Service Truck and Trailer Repairs: As your one-stop solution for truck and trailer repairs, excluding tires, we offer comprehensive services to keep your entire fleet in optimal condition.

Feel free to contact us for more information or to inquire about our services. We look forward to serving you!
